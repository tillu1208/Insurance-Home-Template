Two-Wheeler Insurance

A two-wheeler insurance provides a safety net against theft, fire, natural disasters, malicious acts, etc. Further, in case of third-party injuries or property damage, the third-party liability covers for the same. Also, a bike insurance is mandated by The Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 in India.

About Us

LTI Insurance Company Limited is General Insurance Company. It was incorporated in Mumbai, Maharashtra on December 5 2016.
